About Me

Who is Blythe Williams and Why BigDreamz?


I always had a lot of hobbies and I decided to use those hobbies and do something productive with it. Capturing moments with photography, telling a story with videography. Promoting businesses with graphic design, and giving back to the community with basketball skills training. I have more skills I would love to advertise, but they are still a work in progess.

So, why the name BigDreamz? Honestly, it just has a nice ring to it and it is something I believe we all have. We all have these dreams that even some of us can possibly say that may be too big to reach, unattainable. However, I believe that no matter how big your dream may be, we can always find a way to turn it into a reality. So that is what my business is all about. Using creativity to turn whatever dreams that you have for a project in mind, and turning that into a reality.

A little bit more about me and my experience. I have a B.S. in Software Engineering from the University of Texas at Dallas. I have taken pictures here and there for the past five years or so. I've come to find out I enjoy more of the action photos because of the ability to really capture the athlete in that mode. I also enjoy videography. It's one of my base level skills and still needs some room for improvement, but one thing about me is that I have no problem learning and expanding my horizons. Graphic design is something I have practiced since high school. Whether you need a business card, youtube cover, promote your business, or promote an event. The list goes on, but if there is a graphic needed, I'm the one for you! As for basketball skills training, I have played basketball ever since I was five years old and I played all the way through college. We won a conference championship and I won some individual awards as well. I have a lot of knowledge and my goal is to share that with upcoming hoopers and give back to the basketball community. Helping increase IQ, confidence, and skill set.

Hopefully, after reading all of this, you get a better idea of what I bring to the table. I hope in the future to do business with you soon.
